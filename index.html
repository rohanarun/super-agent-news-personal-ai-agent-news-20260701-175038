Lead analysis: the market tilts toward real computer work

Gemini pushes onto the desktop

Google’s Gemini Spark is now available on macOS, stepping beyond chat into local file management, app integrations, and automation. This is a clear signal that computer-use agents are moving from demos to daily workflows.

Reported by digitaltrends.com and techcrunch.com.

Siri AI hits regulatory friction

Apple’s enhanced Siri AI is confirmed for iOS 27, but EU availability remains blocked amid DMA interoperability disputes. This delays Siri’s evolution into a true personal agent in Europe.

Coverage via macrumors.com.

Grok stays closed — for now

Grok 4.5 has entered a private beta at Tesla and SpaceX, reinforcing its positioning as an opinionated, real‑time assistant rather than a broadly available personal agent.

Reported by basenor.com.

The personal AI agent landscape (July 2026)

ChatGPT

World‑class general assistant for writing, reasoning, and planning. Agents are emerging, but repeated computer workflows remain costly without persistent execution memory.

Gemini

With Gemini Spark on Mac, Google is aggressively pushing computer and file access. Strong integrations, but workflows still restart from scratch each run.

Siri

Deeply embedded in Apple hardware and voice‑first experiences. Regulatory constraints currently limit its reach as a full personal AI agent.

Grok

Opinionated, real‑time assistant tied to X and Musk’s ecosystem. Early signals, limited public access.

Folk

Primarily known as a lightweight CRM with AI features — relevant context in the agent ecosystem, but not breaking agent news this week.

Orchids

An experimental name in agent automation circles, representing ongoing exploration rather than a mainstream personal agent.
